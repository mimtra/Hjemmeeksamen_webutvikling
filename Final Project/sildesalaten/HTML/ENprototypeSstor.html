The development of Jarlife

When we brainstormed potential solutions to the problems we had identified, we also began to sketch on what it could look like. Early on we decided to create an online supermarket. To complete such a site is beyond the scope of this project, but to visualize how the final product might look we decided to prototype our idea and let potential users test the concept. We started by making a few low-fidelity prototypes outlining the general layout of the page. These were hand-drawn sketches and mind-maps identifying which sub-pages we needed and important functions that we wanted to include. From these sketches we developed our favourite design into a mid-fidelity wireframe, by making a mock-up of the page digitally. The wireframe includes the layout of the page, some clickable buttons, sub-pages and stock photos to show how the page will look.
