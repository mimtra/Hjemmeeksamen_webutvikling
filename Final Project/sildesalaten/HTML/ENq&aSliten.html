Q&A

Where will Jarlife be available?

Our plan is to launch the business in urban Norway (Starting in Oslo and Bergen) where the need of reduced traffic is the highest. We see a future where Jarlife will expand and ordering groceries online, picking them up at a local pick-up point will also be an option for people living in the countryside.

Who’s your target group?

Our main target group consists of people that have a tight schedule and would benefit from a quick online solution that also provides door delivery. We specifically want to target people from 25 – 40 years old, an age range that often includes a focus on career and family life with toddlers.

How do you plan to develop and expand?

The advantage of launching this concept in Norway is the high domestic production of groceries. To cooperate with the Norwegian food industry and changing the infrastructure of the production by replacing the paradigm of single-serving containers with reusable containers from Jarlife. This will make a bigger impact on national sustainable development, than it would in countries with a higher food import percentage. Our goal is to develop containers with measures fitting into the production chain of the food industry. For example if Norwegian juice producers would integrate our reusable bottles in the packaging of their products (Landbruk, 2019).
